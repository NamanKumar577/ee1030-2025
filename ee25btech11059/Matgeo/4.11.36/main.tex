\textbf{Question}:
Find the coordinates of the point where the line through the points $(3, -4, -5)$ and $(2, -3, 1)$ crosses the plane determined by the points $(1, 2, 3)$, $(4, 2, -3)$ and $(0, 4, 3)$.

\begin{align}
    \vec{n^T}\vec{x}=1
\end{align}

\begin{align}
\vec{n^T}\vec{P}=1\\
\vec{n^T}\vec{Q}=1\\

\begin{align}
\augvec{3}{1}{
